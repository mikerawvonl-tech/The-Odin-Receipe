Toast ala Mike

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Ham
  • Cheese (Emmentaler or Gouda is the best Choice)
  • Carameliced Onions
  • Dijon Mustard

Preperation

  1. Put a thin layer of Dijon Mustard on the top of the botoom bread slice
  2. On this give a slice of cheese
  3. Add 2 slices of ham
  4. Add a slice of cheese
  5. Add some carameliced onions on the top
  6. Put the top slice of bread on the top
  7. add some butter on top and the bottom side of the toast
  8. Toast it for 5 minutes in the sandwich toaster
  9. Now you can enjoy the best toast of the world and help you to survive
