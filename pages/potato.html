Potato Wedges

Ingredients

  • 500g liitle Potatos
  • Oil (oliveoil is great)
  • Salt

Preperation

  1. Wash the potatos thorougly
  2. Cut them in wedges
  3. Put them in a bowl and add some oil and salt
  4. Mix it thorougly, so that all wedges are covered with oil and salt
  5. Put them on a baking tray with baking paper
  6. Bake them for 25 minutes at 200 degrees celsius
  7. Now you can enjoy the best potato wedges of the world and help you to survive
