Mike`s Burger

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Burger Paddy of Meat or similar
  • Tomatos
  • Cheese (Emmentaler or Gouda is the best Choice)
  • Carameliced Onions
  • Dijon Mustard

Preperation

  1. Grill the Burger Patty to your liking
  2. Put some Dijon Mustard on the bottom side of the burger bun
  3. Add the Patty on top
  4. Add a slice of cheese on top of the Patty
  5. Add some carameliced onions on the top
  6. Add some slices of tomato
  7. Put the top slice of bread on the top
  8. Now you can enjoy the best burger of the world and help you to survive
